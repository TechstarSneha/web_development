🏸 Badminton


I love to play badminton. In my childhood, I really did't knew how to play badminton and thus no one used to play with me just because I was a noob. But later in my 6th class I joined badminton classes. But the fact is I did it just for one month, it isn't the thing that I was not interested but there was no one to drop me at the place and my parents didn't allowed me to go alone so I had to give up going to classes. Also in the classes more focus was on physical workout and very less playing practice. So at last I wasn't able to be good at it. Later I used to play with the children in my locality and slowly developed as a good badminton player. After I was a good enough player, I played badminton at regional level at navodaya. Looking behnid, I realize that it was a great journey. These days I play badminton with my friends at gymkhana courts in IIT.
